The EBIC Story

From multi-disciplinary design to digital construction leadership

EBIC - Ehrig BIM & IT Consultation, Inc. was founded in March 2020 after 13 years of intensive multi-disciplinary design practice. What started as a mechanical engineering career evolved through architecture, aerospace engineering, and software development into a comprehensive digital construction consulting firm.

Mission

To deliver transparent, reliable, and cost-effective digital construction solutions that empower AEC professionals to build better, faster, and smarter.

Our open-source philosophy drives everything we do. We believe in transparent processes, reliable methodologies, and cost-effective solutions that put client success first. This isn't just talk - it's proven through 75+ major projects across aviation, government, commercial, and industrial sectors.

What makes EBIC different is experience from every angle. Having worked as a designer, contractor coordinator, facility owner's representative, and IT systems integrator, I understand the challenges and opportunities from all perspectives. This 360-degree view enables solutions that work for everyone involved in the project lifecycle.

Philosophy

We don't just deliver services - we build partnerships. Every project is an opportunity to transfer knowledge, optimize workflows, and elevate the entire team's capabilities.
